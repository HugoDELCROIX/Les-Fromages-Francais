Description

Muenster, often known as munster, is a semi-soft American cheese. It is supposed to be a copy of the Alsatian washed-rind Munster cheese, which was brought to the United States by German immigrants. It's not to be confused with Sweet Muenster Cheese, a processed dairy product.
